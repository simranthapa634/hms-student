    Meet Our Graduates(TSE)

    These teaching aids were thoughtfully created by students from the Diploma in Early Childhood Development and Education(ECDE), and Diploma in Tamil Studies with Early Education(TSE). The global pandemic has shifted parts of our young children’s learning to home-based learning. The continuity of learning is made possible through these simple, yet meaningful teaching aids that parents can use with their children at the comfort of their home. Through this project, students created teaching aids with everyday resources to empower learning for children alongside their schools and families
