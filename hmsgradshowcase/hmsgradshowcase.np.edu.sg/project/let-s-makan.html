Let’s Makan

You know about our hawker centres, as any good Singaporean does, but how much do you really know about them? Your nearby hawker centre is where you go to get a good plate of char kway teow and chase mynahs away. It is also a place of Singaporean history, culture and heritage - and there are plenty islandwide!


Join us as we uncover how the hawker culture has evolved from hawker carts that plied the streets of Singapura to being inscribed on UNESCO as Singapore's intangible cultural heritage.  Let us take you from Adam Food Centre to Tiong Bahru Food Centre to Tekka Market, from first generation to third-generation hawkers, and from the sweetness of Chendol to the heat of Rendang. ​Follow us as we uncover and explore the transformation and evolution of hawkers from being illegal in the 60s to being our national pride & culture now!
