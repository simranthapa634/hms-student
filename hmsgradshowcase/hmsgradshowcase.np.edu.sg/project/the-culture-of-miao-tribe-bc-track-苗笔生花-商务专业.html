The Culture of Miao Tribe; 苗笔生花

Chinese traditional clothing, such as cheongsam and Hanfu, has become such a fashion trend that China even has a "China Huafu Day", where people roam the streets dressed in traditional costumes. 

For the Miao tribe, their colourful clothing is considered one of their distinctive folk arts. Spinning, weaving, cross-stitching, brocading, wax printing, and sewing are the most common methods used by women to create garments. These garments are separated into two categories: regular wear and holiday attire. The women would also wear silver embellishments for important events. Through our website, we hope to promote the existence of Chinese ethnic costumes, and make more people experience the charm of Chinese ethnic culture and art.
