Insight Out

Our project aims to raise awareness about suicide, suicide prevention and mental health services to youths in Singapore. Ultimately, we believe that by increasing the society's knowledge and awareness about suicide, we will be able to help eliminate the stigma around it.

Our social media content includes understanding suicide, information about mental health services, helping loss survivors and those in distress, as well as suicide prevention tips. Through collaborating with Samaritans of Singapore, we hope to provide a professional outlook and guidance on suicide prevention. We want to promote more open conversations about suicide through authentic stories and perspectives from our beneficiary community and mental health professionals. Most importantly, we hope that more could be empathetic towards suicide attempters and loss survivors.
