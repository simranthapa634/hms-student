Blissful Harmony; 中囍合璧

Our website aims to provide an in-depth look at traditional Chinese weddings, presenting various aspects of this subject such as wedding costumes, customs and culture, so that readers obtain a better understanding of this topic. Furthermore, our website includes related Chinese traditional wedding game, videos, as well as relevant interactive tools. In order to discover the actual customs of the traditional Chinese weddings, we approached a store selling traditional Chinese gifts for an interview. 

The information on our website is presented in both Chinese and English, so as to enable more people to learn about traditional Chinese weddings, and ultimately reach a wider audience.
