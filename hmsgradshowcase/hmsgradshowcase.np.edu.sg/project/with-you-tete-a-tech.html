With You & Téte-a-Tech

Our project, With You, aims to empower seniors to age actively despite the limitations imposed by the Covid-19 safe distancing measures. We came up with the idea of delivering weekly boxes to seniors containing materials for curated activities, covering physical, cognitive, and social maintenance during the Circuit Breaker period. Our team reached out and partnered with Lion Befrienders, St Luke’s Eldercare, AWWA, and Citi-YMCA Youth for Causes for outreach to elderlies and for funding. The team has thus far been able to serve 90 seniors. 

While serving the elderly, our team noticed the plight of the homeless and the destitute during the pandemic. This community was not only exposed and vulnerable, but many of them also lacked access to public and relief services due to their isolation. Our team came up with Téte-a-Tech, and collaborated with New Hope Community Services (NHCS) to conduct digital literacy workshops for residents at the NHCS shelter. These workshops were conducted through face-to-face lessons to equip shelter residents with basic skills to use smart phones. Our mission is to support and encourage the use of technology to include them in the progression of our society.
