Come on Board: Journey of Singapore’s Commute

Come on board a portal of nostalgia, and join Uncle Ah Hock as he takes you through an exploration of the history and development of Singapore’s transportation system over the years. 

 

Upon (digitally) hopping on-board this virtual exhibition, navigate between 3 different journeys to embark on - Bus, MRT or Taxi. Each journey takes on its own unique presentation method that taps on various interactive and multi-sensorial functions to tell its development story. 

 

By tapping into the rich historical archive of Roots.sg and National Archives Singapore, the exhibition captures the essence of old Singapore and what it was like to travel through the hustle and bustle of the streets. We invite visitors to step into the exhibition and dive into the history of Singapore’s land transport system and gain a deeper appreciation of Singapore’s heritage.
