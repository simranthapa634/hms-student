A Magical Journey; 蘑法之旅

In a far-away mysterious magic school, Huanhuan and Banban are two polar opposites who could not see eye to eye. However, one fine day, their teacher tasked them to find a magical mushroom deep within a large forest. Soon, they find themselves on their way to the forest, and Huanhuan and Banban must work together to overcome the dangers of the forest, as they seek high and low for the magical mushroom. We follow the two main characters as they come to realise the merits and strengths of each other, as well as accepting their weaknesses. Through this story, we hope to teach children the meaning of friendship and acceptance. Moreover, we also hope that our project can inspire children to pick up the good habit of reading. 

在一个很远很远的魔法学校里，同学老师们都知道欢欢和斑斑合不来。不过有一天，蝴蝶老师竟然要他们俩到阴暗的森林里寻找友谊蘑菇。为了找到蘑菇，欢欢和斑斑要齐心协力克服森林里的陷阱，同时学会发现以及接受对方的优缺点。我们的故事叙述了两位主人公，随着他们突破重重困难，最终成为好朋友。这个故事主要是让孩子感受到友谊的可贵，以及鼓励他们多阅读华文故事书，养成读书的好习惯。
