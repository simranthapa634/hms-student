NP Supportify

NP Supportify aims to support the SEN community in NP across all schools and diplomas. Our team seeks to improve the inclusivity and accessibility of the school through providing peer support and avenues for self-advocacy for SEN students; and sharing knowledge, skills, values, and habits that can create a more supportive school community.

Currently, we have 50 club members across all NP schools, of which 20% have declared SEN status. On Instagram, we have amassed 180+ followers. Our video series ‘Ask Nicolette’, where our Vice-President shared about her hearing impairment and school life, has been viewed 800 times, and was shared by the SEN counsellors to parents of SEN students at the start of the AY21/22 semester. At one of our playlist sessions where we watched short films with disability representation and had discussions afterwards, one member shared that they “liked the short film screening because it allowed [them] to exercise my empathy in a way that really spoke to [them].”

Our Research and Advocacy arm is working on producing a digital accessibility guide for student groups as well as independent research on SEN student participation.
