Mine Becomes Yours

Mine Becomes Yours is a student-initiated project that crowdfunds and crowdsources for stationery for low-income school-going children, in collaboration with several Family Service Centres (FSC). We started out with a donation campaign within NP, but soon saw the potential of this initiative to go beyond NP, and decided to engage the public through a fundraising campaign on GIVE.ASIA (December 2020 - Current). Support towards the initiative was felt as the public not only donated funds that go towards keeping the initiative running which means being able to collaborate with more FSCs and benefit more students in need, but also actively reached out to us seeking to donate stationery items.

Seeing the success of the stationery runs and with great support received from the public towards the initiative, we realise that Mine Becomes Yours still has the means and capacity to benefit more students in need with stationery packs to support their learning. As such, we are looking to reach out and collaborate with more FSCs to benefit more students in need around Singapore through providing them with stationery items to aid them in their learning as well as other initiatives.
